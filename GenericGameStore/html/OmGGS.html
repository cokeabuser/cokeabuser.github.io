Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I create an account?

Just click the "Login" button in the top right corner and follow the "Register" link on the login page.

❓ Can I buy games without logging in?

No, you must be logged in to add items to your cart and proceed to checkout.

❓ What payment methods are accepted?

We accept most major credit cards and PayPal. More options will be added soon!

❓ I found a bug! What should I do?

Please go to our Support page and report the issue. We appreciate your help!
